XLI. ad Ameanam

Ameana puella defututa
tota milia me decem poposcit,
ista turpiculo puella naso,
decoctoris amica Formiani.
propinqui, quibus est puella curae,
amicos medicosque convocate:
non est sana puella, nec rogare
qualis sit solet aes imaginosum.

XLI. to Ameana

Ameana, the fucked-out moll,
demanded of me a whole ten thousand,
that wench with the ugly nose,
the girlfriend of the sprendthrift Formian.
You next of kin, in whose care she is,
call together your friends and doctors:
The girl's not sane, and it's not her wont
to ask her mirror what shape she's in.
