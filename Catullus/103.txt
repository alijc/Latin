CIII. ad Silonem

Aut sodes mihi redde decem sestertia, Silo,
     deinde esto quamvis saevus et indomitus:
aut, si te nummi delectant, desine quaeso
     leno esse atque idem saevus et indomitus.

To Silus

Please either return the ten thousand sestertia to me, Silus,
  then be as savage and wild as you wish:
or, if the coins delight you, I beg you, stop being
  a pimp, and stop being savage and wild as well.
