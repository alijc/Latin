
I.

Hunc lucum tibi dedico consecroque, Priape,
qua domus tua Lampsaci est quaque ... Priape.
nam te praecipve in suis urbibus colit ora
Hellespontia, ceteris ostriosior oris.

I dedicate and consecrate this grove to you, Priapus,
where your house is in Lampsacius ..., Priapus.
for the shore of the Hellespont chiefly worships you
in its cities, richer in oysters than any other shore.


II.

de meo ligurrire libido est

there is a lust for licking about me


III.

at non effugies meos iambos

but you will not escape my meters
