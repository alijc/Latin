XXVI. ad Furium

Furi villula vestra non ad Austri
flatus opposita est neque ad Favoni
nec saevi Boreae aut Apheliotae,
verum ad milia quindecim et ducentos.
o ventum horribilem atque pestilentem! 

XXVI. to Furius

Furius, yins guys' villa withstands
not the southern breeze or the western wind
or the fierce north wind or the east wind,
but is in hock for a thousand, fifteen and two hundred.
O what an awful and toxic wind.
