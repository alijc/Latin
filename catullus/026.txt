XXVI. ad Furium

Furi villula vestra non ad Austri
flatus opposita est neque ad Favoni
nec saevi Boreae aut Apheliotae,
verum ad milia quindecim et ducentos.
o ventum horribilem atque pestilentem! 

XXVI. to Furius

Furius, you guys's country house is opposed, not to the southern 
breeze or to Favonius???
and not to the fierce north wind or the east wind,
indeed to a thousand, fifteen and two hundred.
O what an awful and unwholesome wind.
