XXVII. ad pincernam suum

Minister vetuli puer Falerni
inger mi calices amariores,
ut lex Postumiae iubet magistrae
ebrioso acino ebriosioris.
at vos quo lubet hinc abite, lymphae
vini pernicies, et ad severos
migrate. hic merus est Thyonianus.

XXVII. to his cupbearer

Servant boy for aged Falernium
bring me more pungent goblets,
as the law of Mistress Postumia dictates
juicier than the grape itself.
But you whom it pleases to leave here, water
ruination of wine, move on to sober men.
Here is pure Bacchus.
