XL. ad Ravidum

Quaenam te mala mens, miselle Ravide,
agit praecipitem in meos iambos?
quis deus tibi non bene advocatus
vecordem parat excitare rixam?
an ut pervenias in ora vulgi?
quid vis? qualubet esse notus optas?
eris, quandoquidem meos amores
cum longa voluisti amare poena.

XL. to Ravidus

Pray, poor Ravidus, what evil mind
did drive you headlong into my meters?
Which god, not well invoked by/for you,
prepares to raise a frenzied brawl?
Can't you arrive at the mob's edge?
What do you want?  Do you choose to be known however you please?
You will, since you desired
to love my lover with a long-lived price.
