XLIII. ad Ameanam

Salve, nec minimo puella naso
nec bello pede nec nigris ocellis
nec longis digitis nec ore sicco
nec sane nimis elegante lingua,
decoctoris amica Formiani.
ten provincia narrat esse bellam?
tecum Lesbia nostra comparatur?
o saeclum insapiens et infacetum!

XLIII. to Ameana

Hail, lass, with neither the smallest nose
or a lovely foot or dark little eyes
or long fingers or a dry mouth
or a tongue of too sound elegance,
girlfriend of the bankrupt Formian.
Does the province say that you are lovely?
Is our Lesbia is compared with you?
O unwise and witless age!
