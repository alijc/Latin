LXXIX. ad Lesbium

Lesbius est pulcer. quid ni? quem Lesbia malit
     quam te cum tota gente, Catulle, tua.
sed tamen hic pulcer vendat cum gente Catullum,
     si tria natorum suavia reppererit.

to Lesbius

Lesbius is handsome.  Why not?  whom Lesbia prefers
     than you with all your people, Catullus.
But nevertheless this handsome man would sell Catullus with people,
     if three kisses of sons he would get.
