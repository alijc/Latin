LXXXI. ad Iuventium

Nemone in tanto potuit populo esse, Iuventi,
     bellus homo, quem tu diligere inciperes.
praeterquam iste tuus moribunda ab sede Pisauri
     hospes inaurata palladior statua,
qui tibi nunc cordi est, quem tu praeponere nobis
     audes, et nescis quod facinus facias?

LXXXI. to Juventius

is no one in such a great populace able to be, Juventius,
  a handsome man, whom you undertake to love.
besides that man of yours from a dying home of Pisaurius
  host ??? with a gilded statue,
who to you now to the heart is, whom you to put before us
  dare, and don't know that you would commit a crime?
