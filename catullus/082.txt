LXXXII. ad Qvintium

Quinti, si tibi vis oculos debere Catullum
     aut aliud si quid carius est oculis,
eripere ei noli, multo quod carius illi
     est oculis seu quid carius est oculis.

LXXXII. to Quintius

Quintius, if to yourself you wish eyes to owe Catullus
  or another thing if what more dear is than eyes,
refuse to snatch away to him, to/by much that deared to him
  is than/to eyes or if what dearer is than/to eyes.
