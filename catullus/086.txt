LXXXVI. ad Lesbiam

Quintia formosa est multis. mihi candida, longa,
     recta est: haec ego sic singula confiteor.
totum illud formosa nego: nam nulla venustas,
     nulla in tam magno est corpore mica salis.
Lesbia formosa est, quae cum pulcerrima tota est,
     tum omnibus una omnis surripuit Veneres.


LXXXVI. to Lesbia

To many, Quintia is beautiful.  To me she is blonde, tall,
  and straight: I so confess these things one by one.
All the same I say no to 'beautiful': for no beauty,
  not a grain of salt is in such a large body.
Lesbia is beautiful, not only the most beautiful of all,
  but also she alone stole all Venuses from everyone else.
