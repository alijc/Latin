LXXXVI. ad Lesbiam

Quintia formosa est multis. mihi candida, longa,
     recta est: haec ego sic singula confiteor.
totum illud formosa nego: nam nulla venustas,
     nulla in tam magno est corpore mica salis.
Lesbia formosa est, quae cum pulcerrima tota est,
     tum omnibus una omnis surripuit Veneres.


LXXXVI. to Lesbia

Quintia is beautiful to many.  To me she is candid, tall
  and honest: these things I so one at a time confess.
that whole thing I deny beautiful: for no grace/charm,
  none in such by/to great is in body grain of salt.
Lesbia is beautiful, who is with a beautiful whole,
  then to/by all one of all stole Venuses.
