XCII. ad Lesbia

Lesbia mi dicit semper male nec tacet umquam
     de me: Lesbia me dispeream nisi amat.
quo signo? quia sunt totidem mea: deprecor illam
     assidue, verum dispeream nisi amo.


XCII. to Lesbia

Lesbia always speaks viciously to me and never shuts up about it:
   Lesbia loves me unless I should perish.
by any mark?  For mine are just the same: I entreat her
   constantly, truly I would perish unless I love.
