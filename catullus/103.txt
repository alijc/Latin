CIII. ad Silonem

Aut sodes mihi redde decem sestertia, Silo,
     deinde esto quamvis saevus et indomitus:
aut, si te nummi delectant, desine quaeso
     leno esse atque idem saevus et indomitus.

To Silus

Either please to me return ten sestertia, Silus,
  from there/then you will be however much fierce and untamable:
or, if coins delight you, stop, I beg you,
  you pimp, to be and also fierce and untamable.
