CIX. ad Lesbiam

Iucundum, mea vita, mihi proponis amorem
hunc nostrum inter nos perpetuumque fore.
di magni, facite ut vere promittere possit,
atque id sincere dicat et ex animo,
ut liceat nobis tota perducere vita
aeternum hoc sanctae foedus amicitiae.

to Lesbia

You show delightful love, my life, to me
that this love between us will be ours and eternal.
Great gods! make it so really she can promise,
and she might say it purely and from her heart,
so that it is permitted to us a whole life to lead
this eternal alliance of sacred friendship.
