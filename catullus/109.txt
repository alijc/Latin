CIX. ad Lesbiam

Iucundum, mea vita, mihi proponis amorem
hunc nostrum inter nos perpetuumque fore.
di magni, facite ut vere promittere possit,
atque id sincere dicat et ex animo,
ut liceat nobis tota perducere vita
aeternum hoc sanctae foedus amicitiae.

to Lesbia

You proclaim to me, my life, that this delightful love
between us will be eternal and our own.
Great gods! make her able to prophesy truly,
and let her say it sincerely and from her heart,
and grant that all our lives we will prolong
this eternal bond of sacred friendship.
