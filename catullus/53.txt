LIII. ad Gaium Licinium Calvum

Risi nescio quem modo e corona,
qui, cum mirifice Vatiniana
meus crimina Caluos explicasset
admirans ait haec manusque tollens,
'di magni, salaputium disertum!'

LIII. to Gaius Licinius Calvus

I laughed, I don't know whom merely from the crown,
who, when amazingly Vatiniana
had explained my man crimes to Calvuses 
admiring he says these hands lifting,
'Great gods! eloquent little man!'
