The Antelope

There is a certain animal that is called an antelope; because when it plays with brushwood with its horns, in the end its horns are so tangled with the brushwood that it can't pull them out. And then it begins to cry out. And when the hunters hear this they come and kill it.

Moral. It so happened that a good many were delighted and play with the troubles of this world and are so tangled with them that they can not be pulled out. And so are seized and killed by the hunters, that is, by demons.
