The Panther

The panther is a certain animal that discharges an odor so that savage animals, like the lion, the wolf and the leopard, who are unwilling to harm the panther, upon sensing this odor, immediately become gentle and no longer attack it.

Moral. There are some who are so kind in words and deeds so that even their enemies, on hearing them, soothed by their sweet speech, sweep away their rage and hatred and esteem them. From which Solomon in an allegory: "A soft answer turneth away wrath; a harsh word rouses anger"; and again: "A good word is better than a gift."
