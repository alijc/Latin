The Rasher of Bacon That Was Hanging in a Certain Village

I once passed through a certain village in France, where they'd hung a ham or bacon in the village square, with the condition that anyone who wished to swear an oath that he and his wife had passed an entire year after their marriage without being dissatisfied with the marriage could have the bacon. And when it had hung there for ten years not even one person was found who won the bacon, with everyone repenting of their marriage within a year.
