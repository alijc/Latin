Vergil and Lucinius

START

AJC There also blossomed during the time of Rome that most renowned poet Virgil, who beginning from Mantua, a city of Sicily, was quite well known to the king, for he had even been honored by him in his services.

AJC To him therefore, on account of his acquaintance and because the particular one had then of time among philosophers, when/as to/by great services a father sent his son, entreating him by his gods that his knowledge might prepare how far/long the boy and he might take heed of spiteful treachery more carefully.

AJC For the father feared lest he suffer anything of the left hand through hatred, for he had learned this from the gods and from those expert of the stars.

AJC But Virgil, on account of the respect and friendship of the king, taking the boy in, indeed at first related to him the rudiments of letters and then about to flatter and mollify, as is the custom of teachers, to construct a syllable from letters and to form from syllables a speech and from speeches indeed to execute an oration he throroughly taught him in the time of Augustus.
