The Treasure

START

AJC In olden days there was a certain king, great and powerful.

AJC And he, taking great care to collect his treasures, filled a tower of great height and breadth to the top with gold and silver and all sorts of precious things.

AJC Now this man had a soldier who had been proven loyal in many things, and he entrusted the keys to his treasure to him.

AJC But when the soldier accepted the position of protector of the treasure, since he had already rolled through many years and had been weakened by toil and old age and would no longer be able to deal with the bustle of a court post, resolutely asked the king in turn to show consideration for his debility and old age and take back the keys to his treasure, and allow him to retire to his own home and spend the remaining span of his life quietly and agreeably among his sons.

AJC Indeed, the king, reflecting on what he might reasonably and of necessity ask, after presenting him with great gifts, nevertheless sadly permitted him to depart.

AJC And so, after taking the keys and the treasure back, he entrusted them a second time to the protection of another.

AJC But when the soldier came to his dear home, worry was hanging over himself and his family.
