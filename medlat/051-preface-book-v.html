PREFACE TO BOOK V

START

AJC It tires me to remember the details of the civil wars that are crushing the people and kingdom of the Franks; in which we already see what is evil, the beginning of the time that the Lord foretold in sorrow: when father rises up against son, son against father, brother against brother, neighbor against neighbor.

AJC For the examples should have frightened the kings of old, who, as they were divided, were at once destroyed by their enemies.

AJC As often as both the very city of cities and the head of the whole world, in entering into civil wars, is razed; it heedlessly rises again, as if out of the earth.

AJC If only even you, o kings, would drill yourselves, in these battles in which your parents exerted themselves, like nations frightened by your union, they would be hard-pressed by your power.

AJC Remember that the source of your victories was Clovis, who killed the kings who fought him, crushed guilty nations, subjugated lands, and from these left you a kingdom whole and unharmed.

AJC And when he did this he had neither the gold nor the silver that's now in your treasuries.

AJC What are you doing?

AJC What are you seeking?

AJC What are you lacking?

AJC In your houses the luxuries multiply, in your storerooms the wine, wheat and oil overflow, in your treasuries the gold and silver are heaped up.
