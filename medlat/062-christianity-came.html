HOW CHRISTIANITY CAME TO BRITAIN

START

AJC Nor should we pass over in silence the story which was carried down to us from the blessed Gregory in the tradition of our ancestors.

AJC They say that on a certain day when, as the traders had recently arrived, many things had been brought together for sale in the market and many people had assembled to buy, and Gregory himself came with the others, and saw, among other things for sale, boys set of radiant body and graceful mien, and of hair with extraordinary beauty.

AJC And as he gazed on them he asked, so they say, which region or land they had been brought from.

AJC And he was told that they were from the island of Britain, whose inhabitants were of such an appearance.

AJC Again he asked whether these islanders were Christian, or were still entangled in pagan errors.

AJC He was told that they were pagans.

AJC But he, drawing a sigh from his inmost heart, said, "Alas, what sorrow! that the author of darkness is master of men of such clear faces and that so fair an outside carries a mind free of inward grace."

AJC And so he asked once more what the name of that tribe was.

AJC The answer was that they were called Angles.

AJC But he said, "Good, for they even have angelic faces and it is fitting for such men to be co-heirs of angels in heaven.

AJC What name has that province, from which these were brought?"

AJC The answer was that the province was called Deiri.

AJC But he said, "Good, Deiri; from wrath they were uprooted, and were called to the mercy of Christ.

AJC How is the king of that province called?"

AJC The answer was that he is called Aelli.

AJC But he, making a play on the name, said: "Alleluia, it is meet that the praise of God the creator be sung in those parts."
