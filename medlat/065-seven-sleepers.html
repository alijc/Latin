The Seven Sleepers

START

AJC I by no means think that it is from the matter to postpone for a short while for relating the series, and since thus far a stylus is engaged in Germany, a miracle, because there it is frequently held with all men, and also to briefly recount certain other things.

AJC In the furthest northwest region of Germany, on the very ocean shore a cave can be seen under a towering cliff, where seven men drowse. They were put into a deep sleep God knows how long ago and are whole, in body but also in their garments, as from this very thing, because they have lasted through the course of so many years without decay, they are held in veneration by ignorant nations and also barbarians.

AJC In the end, when one looks closely at their dress, these men are seen to be Romans.

AJC And if someone were greedy enough to try to undress one of these, it is said that his arms immediately wither, and his punishment terrifies the rest, so that none dare to touch them further.

AJC Look at what an accomplishment divine providence has preserved through so many seasons.

AJC Perhaps of these whenever, since not otherwise unless Christians to be they are believed, these Gentiles by proclamation about to be saved are.
