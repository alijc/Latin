The Vision of Gunthramnus

START

AJC And one fact about him pleases us as remarkable enough to briefly insert into our account, especially since we know that it was very poorly preserved in the history of the Franks.

AJC Once when in hunting he entered a forest, and as usual, while his companions wandered about hither and yon, he himself stayed with a single faithful retainer. He was weighed down by a deep slumber, and laying his head into his faithful man's lap, he fell asleep.

AJC And a small animal, rather like a reptile, came out of his mouth and began to bustle about a small rivulet that flowed nearby, trying to cross it.

AJC Then that same man whose lap he was resting drew his broadsword from its scabbard and placed it over that rivulet; and that reptile, about which we spoke, travelled across it to the other side.

AJC And a little while later, after it had entered a certain fissure in the mountain and returned after some time, it crossed over the rivulet on the aforementioned sword, and reentered Gunthramnus' mouth, from which it had issued before.
