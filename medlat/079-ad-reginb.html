To Reginb...

AJC To my dearest Reg..., Lupus sends greetings in the Lord.

AJC As is fit, we certainly long for your arrival, which certain letters have already promised; but we urge that a prudent route be chosen with a watchful care, because in the realm of our King Charles, with new things rising, robberies are committed with impunity, and there is nothing surer or more constant than that the violence of robberies is happening more often.

AJC Therefore, for such a visitation a fellowship must needs be sought, by whose size and strength a party of robbers is avoided, or, if needs must, is driven off.

AJC I pray that you condescend to bring us Sallust's "Catiline Conspiracy" and "Jugurthine War" and the books "Against Verres" and any others that you are aware of, either that we have that are damaged or that we don't have at all, to set right, by your kindness, both the faulty and the missing ones, and never to be had except through you, they are obtained here more pleasantly by which more unexpectedly.

AJC I wish that you'll be healthy and happy.
