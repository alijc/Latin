>AJC

To Hincmarus

AJC To the most illustrious Bishop Hincmarus, Lupus wishes eternal salvation.

AJC I was afraid to direct Bede's commentary on the apostle from Augustine's works, because the book is so large that it can neither be concealed in one's bosom, nor can it be comfortably contained in a satchel; although, if either happened, one must fear the hostile rapacity of brigands, that the beauty of the codex itself would assuredly arouse, and so perhaps it would be destroyed both for me and for you.

AJC Hence I myself mean to securely lend you the recounted volume as soon as, God willing, we may safely meet somewhere.

AJC Now I have undertaken to send as many pine nuts as the courier can take, which is ten.

AJC Let our courier, the same one who carries this (he freed me of an impediment), as you proposed, have your reply as soon as you write it, so that in reading it I might be either instructed or amused.

AJC Please think of me kindly - I wish you to fare happily.

END
