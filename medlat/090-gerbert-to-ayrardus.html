AJC Gerbert, a Former Scholar, to his Pupil Ayrardus, Greetings

AJC We grant your petitions, and admonish you to carry out our work as if it were your own.

AJC The Pliny is amended and the Eugraphius recovered, and the books that were in Orbais and at St Basle are transcribed.

AJC Do what we ask, and we'll do the same for you.

END
