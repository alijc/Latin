AJC To Adalberon, the archbishop of Rheims

AJC What I could have done concerning your work in Mantua I will explain more in person in speech than in writing from a distance.

AJC I don't know where the keys of the books that I left behind are because they all got jumbled together.

AJC Pray obtain the history of Julius Caesar from his lordship Azonus, the abbot of Dervensus, and write back to inform us, so that you may have those that we have for yourselves and you may hope for those that we later found, that is, 8 volumes of Boetius on astrology, also the very famous one on the forms of geometry, and others no less admirable.

AJC Only your absence disturbs our prosperity, day and night.
