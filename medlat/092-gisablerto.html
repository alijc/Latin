To Abbot Gisalbert

If you are well then we rejoice.

We believe that your need is ours as well. We pray that you reveal how we suffer.

The philosopher Demosthenes produced a book on the illnesses of the eyes and their remedies, which he titled "Opthalmicus". We would like the beginning if you have it, and likewise the end of Cicero's "On King Deiotarus".
