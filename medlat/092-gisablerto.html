AJC To Abbot Gisalbert

AJC If you are well then I rejoice.

AJC We believe that your need is ours as well.

AJC We pray that you reveal how we suffer.

AJC The philosopher Demosthenes produced a book on the illnesses of the eyes and their remedies, which he titled "Opthalmicus".

AJC We would like the beginning if you have it, and likewise the end of Cicero's "On King Deiotarus".
