AJC To Hugh from His One-time Teacher Gerbert

AJC My lord has enriched me, according to the breadth of my soul, with the greatest honors.

AJC For which part of Italy does not hold on to the blessed Columbanus' property?

AJC Indeed this is so due to the largess and benevolence of our Caesar.

AJC Truly, Fortune decided otherwise.

AJC Of course, it has honored me, according to the breadth of my soul, with the greatest enemies.

AJC For which part of Italy does not hold my enemies?

AJC My powers are unequal to those of Italy.

AJC The condition of peace is this: if I bear being plundered, they won't kill me.

AJC If I demand my own, they'll pursue me with swords.

AJC When they can't kill me with a sword, they'll assail me with darts of words.

AJC The imperial majesty is scorned, not only in regards to me but even against its very self in the distribution of a sanctuary of God.

AJC Because I am unwilling to consent to the things done according to the leases, I'm labelled a cruel tyrant.

AJC Caesar himself, the most excellent of all men, is regarded as an ass by the scoundrels.

AJC O most faithful of friends, pray you not desert a friend with counsel and aid.

AJC Remember what I told you, that I'd prefer to be a soldier in Caesar's camp than a king outside of it.

END
