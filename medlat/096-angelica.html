An Angel's Voice on the Resurrection of Christ

Whom do you seek in the tomb, o Christians?

The response of the holy women: The crucified Jesus of Nazarus, o heavenly one.

The consolation of the angel's voice: He is not here, he has risen, as was foretold. Go, announce it, saying that he has risen.

The song of the holy women to the whole clergy: Alleluia! Our Lord has risen today, the mighty Lion, Christ the son of God! Give thanks to God, yes!

Let the angel say: Come and see the place where the Lord had been laid, alleluia! alleluia!

Again, let the angel say: Swiftly go and tell the disciples that the Lord has risen, alleluia! alleluia!

Let the women sing with one voice, joyfully: The Lord has risen from the tomb, He who was hung on the cross for our sakes, alleluia!
