AJC The Service for the Star

AJC Let the first one say: A star glows red with too much brilliance.

AJC Let the second one say: Which points out a child, the king of kings.

AJC The third: Whose coming the prophecies of old have indicated.

AJC The first: Come.

AJC The second: Come.

AJC The third: Come, let us honor him, for he himself is the Lord our God.

AJC Then let them join themselves likewise and say: Therefore let us go and seek him, offering to him gifts, gold, frankincense and myrrh, for we know what was written: "All kings will honor him, all peoples will serve him."

AJC The messenger to Herod: Hail, king of the Jews. Lo, wise men come and, led by a star, seek a child, the king of kings.

AJC Herod to the messenger: order them to come before me, so I can know all, Who they are, why they come, by what hearsay do they seek us.

AJC The messenger to the wise men: Royal mandates summon you; do not go half-heartedly.

AJC The wise men to Herod: May the powerful king of the Isrealites live forever.

AJC Herod to the wise men: Whom do you seek, foreigners?

AJC The wise men: We seek a child, the king of the Jews.

AJC Herod: By which sign did you learn who the child-king that you seek is?

AJC The wise men: We learned that this was the child, with the star showing us in the east.

AJC Herod: If you believe that this one will reign, tell us.

AJC The wise men: Acknowledging that this one will reign, We have come from a far off land to honor him with mysterious gifts, Revering the triple God with three gifts.

AJC Let the first say: With gold I honor the king.

AJC The second: With frankincense I honor the priest.

AJC The third: With myrrh I honor the human.
