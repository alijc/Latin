A BURIAL SERVICE

START

AJC After the mass the priest, wearing an alb without a silk cassock, should come up to the head of the deceased; and two clerics of the second rank standing at the head of the deceased should begin a three-fold antiphon, which the choir should follow one time up to the end.

AJC Response: Groans of death enveloped me, the pains of hell enveloped me.

AJC Then after the third repetition should follow:

AJC Lord have mercy. Christ have mercy, Lord have mercy.

AJC Do not say the Lord's prayer, nor 'Lord be with you', nor 'Let us pray', but only the Oratio, with the priest speaking in a restrained voice, that is, without musical notes:

AJC Do not enter into judgment with your servant, Lord, because no man will be justified in your presence, unless he is granted forgiveness for all his sins through you.

AJC Therefore we ask that your judicial sentence not overwhelm him, whom the true supplication of Christian faith commends to you, but with the aid of your grace let him escape the judgment of retribution, he who, while living, was marked with the seal of the holy Trinity.

AJC Through Christ our Lord. Amen.

AJC All of the following prayers are said in the same way. Then the cantor should begin the responsory:

AJC You who resuscitated Lazarus from the stinking tomb, Lord, give them rest.

AJC And a place of indulgence.

AJC Give them eternal rest, Lord. And a place.

END
