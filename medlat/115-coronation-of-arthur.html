The Coronation of Arthur

START

AJC Finally, when everyone had assembled in the city, with pressing solemnity, the archbishops were summoned to the palace to crown the king with the royal diadem.

AJC And so Dubricius, since the court was held in his diocese. ready to perform the ritual, undertook this task for the king.

AJC After some time, the king, dressed in his royal robes, was elaborately conducted to the church of the metropolitan's see; for on his left and his right, two archbishops supported him.

AJC Now four kings, namely, of Scotland and Cornwall, Demetia and Venedocia, whose right it was, preceded him, bearing four golden swords.

AJC Likewise, an choir of those who had taken holy orders was performing wonderful singing and playing.

AJC While in another part archbishops and bishops conducted the queen, adorned with her own ensigns, to the church of maidens dedicated to God.

AJC Likewise the four queens of the four aforesaid kings carried four white doves before her, as was the custom.

AJC And the women who were there followed her with the greatest joy.

AJC Then, when the procession was over, so many kinds of music, so many songs arose in both churches that the soldiers who were there were overwhelmed with the sweetness and couldn't decide which of the churches they should make for.

AJC And so, they swarmed now to this one, now to that; and though the whole day had been given to the celebration, they felt only a little weariness.

AJC At last, after divine services were celebrated in both churches, the king and queen took off their crowns and put on lighter trappings and walked to the feast, he to his palace with the men, she to another with the women.
