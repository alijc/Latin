AJC And Simon followed him, and everyone who was with him.

AJC And when they'd found him, they said to him: Because they are all seeking you.

AJC And he said to them: Let us go to the neighboring villages and towns, so that I might preach there, for this is why I came.

AJC And he was preaching in their synagogues, and in all of Galilee, and casting out demons.

AJC And a leper came to him, begging him: and on bended knee said to him: If thou wishes, thou can make me clean.

AJC Now Jesus felt compassion for him, and stretched out his hand, and touching him, said to him: I so wish: be clean.

AJC And as soon as he had spoken, at once the leprosy withdrew from the man, and he was cleansed.

AJC And he rebuked him, and discharged him at once,

AJC and said to him: See that thou tell no one: but go, show yourself to the head priest, and present for thy cleansing what Moses commanded, in testimony of these things.

AJC But when that one had gone out he began to proclaim, and to spread news of the speech, so that Jesus could no longer openly enter a town, but stayed outside in deserted places, and people would come to him from all directions.
